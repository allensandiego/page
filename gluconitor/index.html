About Gluconitor

Gluconitor is a comprehensive health-tracking application designed to empower individuals with diabetes to monitor their glucose levels, nutrition, physical activity, and medication in one seamless experience. Available for Android, and Web.

Key Features

Comprehensive Health Tracking

Glucose Logging

Track blood sugar readings with specialized meal categories (Before Meal, After Meal, Fasting, etc.).

Nutrition Management

Detailed food logging including Carbohydrates, Protein, Fat, and Calories to monitor glycemic impact.

Exercise Journal

Log activities with duration and intensity levels to monitor your daily activity.

Medication Records

Keep a precise history of insulin or oral medication dosages, including units and specific timing.

Insights & Analytics

At-a-glance Dashboard

Unified view of your latest glucose reading and a trend chart of your most recent data.

Chronological Timeline

A beautiful, filterable list of every health event you've logged in perfect order.

Native Ad Integration

Non-intrusive ads that support the free nature of the platform.

Data Management & Portability

Multi-Format Export

Export your data to CSV, JSON, or Excel (XLSX) for personal backup or use in external tools.

Doctor's Report (PDF)

Generate professional, formatted PDF summaries with custom date ranges and clinical notes to share with your healthcare provider.

Security & Privacy

Secure Authentication

Multi-provider login support including Google, Facebook, Microsoft, and standard Email/Password.

Full Data Sovereignty

A permanent account deletion tool that performs a secure wipe of all personal data from Firestore before account closure.

Privacy-First

Integrated Privacy Policy and Terms of Service accessible directly within the app.
