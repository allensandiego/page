Privacy Policy for Chained

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Chained ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Chained when you play our Android game.

By using Chained, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

The words of which the initial letter is capitalized have meanings defined under the following conditions. The following definitions shall have the same meaning regardless of whether they appear in singular or in plural.

  • Application means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named Chained.
  • Company refers to Chained (Allen Sandiego).
  • Service refers to the Application.
  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we do not require you to provide us with any personally identifiable information. The Application is offline-ready and privacy-focused.

Gameplay Data

Our game involves connecting letter tiles on a grid to form words. All gameplay progress (scores, levels, high scores) and gameplay states are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any keystrokes, word selections, or gameplay data to external servers.

Audio & Sound Effects

The game utilizes the device's audio output to play sound effects via the Android SoundPool API. No audio recording or microphone access is requested or used.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not share any gameplay data with third parties.

4. Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers to store your personal data. Local game data remains on your device until the application is uninstalled or its data is cleared by the user.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

6. Security

We value your trust in using our Application, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any local data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
