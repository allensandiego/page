A native Android client for your Shinobi Video surveillance server.

Download on Play Store

About Peek

Peek is a dedicated Android application designed to provide a smooth and efficient way to monitor your Shinobi Video cameras. It offers a fast, native experience for viewing live streams, reviewing recordings, and managing your surveillance system from anywhere.

Key Features

Live Monitoring

High-performance live streaming of your camera feeds with low latency.

Recording Playback

Easily browse and view recorded events from your Shinobi server.

Multi-Server Support

Configure and switch between multiple Shinobi Video instances with ease.

Secure Access

Connects directly to your server using secure API keys and encrypted protocols.

Security & Privacy

Privacy-First

Read our Privacy Policy. All data stays between your device and your server.
