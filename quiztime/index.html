About Quiz Time

Quiz Time is a fast-paced trivia game designed to challenge your wits across various categories. Whether you're a history buff, a science geek, or a pop culture fanatic, Quiz Time offers endless hours of educational entertainment.

Key Features

Diverse Categories

Choose from a wide range of topics including Geography, Science, Arts, and Sports.

Challenging Levels

Progress through increasingly difficult questions to test the limits of your knowledge.

Timed Gameplay

Think fast! Beat the clock to score higher and climb the leaderboards.

Simple Interface

Enjoy a clean and intuitive design focused on smooth, distraction-free gaming.
