About Xtreamer Remote

Xtreamer Remote provides a convenient way to control your Xtreamer media players directly from your Android device. It mimics the physical remote control, offering all the essential functions over your local network for a more responsive and modern experience.

Key Features

Full Remote Emulation

Access all the buttons and functions of your physical Xtreamer remote control.

Network Connectivity

Seamlessly connect to your media player over your local Wi-Fi network.

Responsive UI

Fast and intuitive interface designed for quick navigation and media control.

Multi-Device Support

Easily manage and switch between multiple Xtreamer players in your home.

Security & Privacy

Privacy-First

Read our Privacy Policy. No data is collected or transmitted outside your local network.
