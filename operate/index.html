About Operate

Operate is a dedicated Android client for TrueNAS servers, designed to give you full control and monitoring capabilities on the go. Whether you need to check system health, manage storage pools, or control services, Operate provides a seamless mobile experience for TrueNAS administrators.

Key Features

System Monitoring

Real-time tracking of CPU, RAM, and network usage to keep your server running optimally.

Storage Management

View and manage your ZFS pools, datasets, and snapshots with ease.

Service Control

Quickly start, stop, or restart TrueNAS services and jails directly from your phone.

Secure Connection

All communications are encrypted and occur directly between your device and your server.
