About Vrooomed

Vrooomed is a modern car management application designed to help users track their vehicle's lifecycle. From fueling records to maintenance logs, keep everything organized in one secure place.

Key Features

Car Management

Add, update, and manage details for multiple vehicles.

Service Logs

Track maintenance history and repairs to keep your vehicle in top condition.

Fuel Tracking

Monitor fuel efficiency and costs over time.

Secure Storage

Data is encrypted and securely stored for your peace of mind.

Multi-Platform Access

Access your data seamlessly via Web or Android devices.

  • Gin Framework
  • PostgreSQL
  • Flyway
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Angular (TypeScript)
