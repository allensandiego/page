Privacy Policy for QuizTime

Last updated: January 11, 2026

QuizTime ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by QuizTime when you play our Android game.

By using QuizTime, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Interpretation and Definitions

The words of which the initial letter is capitalized have meanings defined under the following conditions. The following definitions shall have the same meaning regardless of whether they appear in singular or in plural.

  • Application means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named QuizTime.
  • Company refers to QuizTime (Allen Sandiego).
  • Service refers to the Application.
  • You means the individual accessing or using the Service.

2. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information.

Google Play Games Services

QuizTime uses Google Play Games Services to enhance your gaming experience. This includes:

  • Google Sign-in: We use Google Sign-in to authenticate your identity. When you sign in, we may access your public profile information, such as your display name and player ID.
  • Leaderboards and Achievements: We may submit your high scores and unlocked achievements to Google Play Games Services. This data may be visible to other players based on your privacy settings in the Google Play Games app.

Gameplay Data

Our game involves trivia questions where users guess answers using an in-game custom keyboard. We do not use the system keyboard for these inputs. We do not collect, store, or transmit any keystrokes or text entered via this custom keyboard. Your gameplay progress (e.g., levels completed, coins earned) is stored locally on your device or synced via Google Play Games Services to ensure game continuity.

4. Data Retention

We do not operate our own servers to store your personal data. Any data synced via Google Play Games is subject to Google's data retention policies. Local game data remains on your device until the application is uninstalled or data is cleared.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
